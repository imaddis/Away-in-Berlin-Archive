95% of this archive was captured on a Samsung Galaxy S21+. It went everywhere — to clubs, museums, cafes, flea markets, train stations, and rooftops. It captured the mundane and the memorable without distinction, which is perhaps why the archive feels the way it does.

The phone has since been retired. Some of the files it captured transferred cleanly. Others didn't survive the journey through platforms, uploads, and time. What remains is what remains.
