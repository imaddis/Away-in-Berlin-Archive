About ▾

Away in Berlin is a personal archive documenting a three-month study away experience in Berlin, Germany in 2023. This collection spans photography, video, and screenshots — capturing meals, nights out, landmarks, studio life, and quiet moments in between. Many of these images exist only as degraded Instagram screenshots, their quality a byproduct of the platforms through which they were shared and saved. This archive is both a record and a meditation on digital memory, decay, and what survives.
